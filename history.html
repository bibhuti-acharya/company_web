Our History

ClickMart India Pvt Ltd was founded in 2010 with the vision of revolutionizing the e-commerce industry in India. Starting as a small startup, the company aimed to provide a seamless online shopping experience to customers across the country.

Over the years, ClickMart expanded its operations, introducing innovative technologies and a wide range of products to cater to the diverse needs of Indian consumers. By 2015, the company had established itself as one of the leading e-commerce platforms in India.

In 2020, ClickMart achieved a major milestone by launching its own logistics network, ensuring faster and more reliable deliveries. The company also focused on empowering small and medium businesses by providing them with a platform to reach a larger audience.

Today, ClickMart India Pvt Ltd continues to grow, driven by its commitment to customer satisfaction, innovation, and excellence. With millions of satisfied customers and a dedicated team, the company remains at the forefront of India's e-commerce landscape.
